Best Destinations in Indonesia

Explore the best tourist destinations in Indonesia with our tailored packages or choose your own adventure with our ala carte options.

Packages

Bali Package: Enjoy a 5-day, 4-night stay in Bali, including guided tours to Uluwatu Temple, Tanah Lot, and Ubud's Monkey Forest.

Yogyakarta Package: Experience the rich culture of Yogyakarta with a 4-day, 3-night package, including visits to Borobudur Temple, Prambanan Temple, and the Sultan's Palace.

Lombok Package: Discover the beauty of Lombok with a 5-day, 4-night package, including tours to the Gili Islands, Mount Rinjani, and Kuta Beach.

Ala Carte

Komodo Island: Explore the unique wildlife and pristine beaches of Komodo Island, home to the famous Komodo dragons.

Raja Ampat: Dive into the crystal-clear waters of Raja Ampat, a paradise for divers with its diverse marine life and vibrant coral reefs.

Mount Bromo: Witness the breathtaking sunrise over Mount Bromo, an active volcano set within the stunning Tengger Semeru National Park.
